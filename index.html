ABOUT ME

     When I was 5,  I was a Lego Engineer.

            By that,  I don’t mean I was an official engineer for Lego, even though with just 5 years of age, 

            I’d like to think I was very close to being qualified. Rather I was a Lego fanatic, getting my 

            hands on every brick I could find. The idea of taking materials and turning it into something 

            new or different was something that fascinated me back then and sticks with me today. I remember 

            family members handing me cardboard boxes because they knew I would put it to better use than them 

            throwing it out. Ever since I was a child,  it was written in my code,  I was meant to be an Engineer.
